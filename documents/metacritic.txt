=== MOVIE: The Fast and the Furious ===
YEAR: 2001
SOURCE: Metacritic
SOURCE_URL: https://www.metacritic.com/

## Scores
Metacritic aggregates professional critic reviews (0-100) and user scores (0-10). Individual excerpts vary by film.

## Critic Reviews
Critics often praise Fast Five and Furious 7 as franchise high points. Tokyo Drift and 2 Fast 2 Furious receive more mixed professional scores.

## User Reviews
Users polarize on spectacle-heavy entries (F9, Fate). Fast Five and Tokyo Drift have vocal fan defenders ranking them among the best.
