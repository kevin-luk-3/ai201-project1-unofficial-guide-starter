=== The Fast and the Furious (2001) ===

REVIEW | Wikipedia Critical Response
Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Fast_and_the_Furious_(2001_film)
id="Critical_response">Critical response On Rotten Tomatoes , the film has an approval rating of 55% based on 155 reviews, and an average rating of 5.40/10. The website's critical consensus reads: "Sleek and shiny on the surface, The Fast and the Furious recalls those cheesy teenage exploitation flicks of the 1950s." [ 38 ] On Metacritic , the film has a weighted average score of 58 out of 100 based on 29 critics, indicating "mixed or average reviews".

=== The Fast and the Furious: Tokyo Drift (2006) ===

REVIEW | Wikipedia Critical Response
Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Fast_and_the_Furious:_Tokyo_Drift
id="Critical_response">Critical response The Fast and the Furious: Tokyo Drift gained a 38% approval rating on Rotten Tomatoes based on reviews from 141 critics; the average rating is 5/10. The site's consensus reads: "Eye-popping driving sequences coupled with a limp story and flat performances make this Drift a disappointing follow-up to previous Fast and Furious installments." [ 35 ] On Metacritic , the film has a weighted average score of 45 out of 100 based on reviews from 32 critics, indicating "mixed or average reviews".

=== Fast & Furious (2009) ===

REVIEW | Wikipedia Critical Response
Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fast_&_Furious_(2009_film)
id="Critical_response">Critical response On the review aggregator website Rotten Tomatoes , 28% of 177 critics' reviews are positive, with an average rating of 4.6/10. The website's consensus reads: "While Fast and Furious features the requisite action and stunts, the filmmakers have failed to provide a competent story or compelling characters." [ 31 ] Metacritic , which uses a weighted average , assigned the film a score of 46 out of 100, based on 28 critics, indicating "mixed or average" reviews.
