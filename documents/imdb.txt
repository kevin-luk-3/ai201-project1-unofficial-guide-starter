=== MOVIE: The Fast and the Furious ===
YEAR: 2001
SOURCE: IMDb
SOURCE_URL: https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0232500/

## Trivia
Point Break-inspired undercover cop setup. Real street racers consulted on car culture.

## Production Facts
Directed by Rob Cohen. Inspired by a Vibe magazine article on illegal street racing. Shot in Los Angeles with practical car stunts.

## Casting Notes
Vin Diesel as Dominic Toretto, Paul Walker as Brian O'Conner, Michelle Rodriguez as Letty Ortiz, Jordana Brewster as Mia Toretto.

---

=== MOVIE: 2 Fast 2 Furious ===
YEAR: 2003
SOURCE: IMDb
SOURCE_URL: https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0322259/

## Trivia
Only mainline sequel without Vin Diesel until spinoffs. Tej Parker introduced.

## Production Facts
Directed by John Singleton. Franchise's only main entry without Dom Toretto.

## Casting Notes
Paul Walker, Tyrese Gibson as Roman Pearce, Eva Mendes, Ludacris as Tej Parker. Vin Diesel does not appear.

---

=== MOVIE: The Fast and the Furious: Tokyo Drift ===
YEAR: 2006
SOURCE: IMDb
SOURCE_URL: https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0463985/

## Trivia
Han Lue named after Han Solo. Sung Kang later became franchise fixture.

## Production Facts
Justin Lin's franchise debut. First film set outside the US. Introduces Han, a fan-favorite character.

## Casting Notes
Lucas Black as Sean Boswell, Sung Kang as Han Lue, Bow Wow, Brian Tee as DK. Vin Diesel cameo at end.

---

=== MOVIE: Fast & Furious ===
YEAR: 2009
SOURCE: IMDb
SOURCE_URL: https://www.imdb.com/title/tt1013752/

## Trivia
Gal Gadot's Gisele introduced. Letty's death sets up later resurrection arc.

## Production Facts
Justin Lin directs. Deliberate franchise soft reboot reuniting original cast.

## Casting Notes
Vin Diesel, Paul Walker, Michelle Rodriguez, Jordana Brewster reunited. Gal Gadot debuts as Gisele Yashar.

---

=== MOVIE: Fast Five ===
YEAR: 2011
SOURCE: IMDb
SOURCE_URL: https://www.imdb.com/title/tt1596343/

## Trivia
Luke Hobbs introduced; Dwayne Johnson joined after negotiating with Universal. Vault scene used real trucks.

## Production Facts
Pivoted franchise from street racing to heist action. Vault chase filmed in Puerto Rico doubling for Rio.

## Casting Notes
Vin Diesel, Paul Walker, Dwayne Johnson debuts as Luke Hobbs, Jordana Brewster, Ludacris, Tyrese Gibson, Sung Kang as Han, Gal Gadot.

---

=== MOVIE: Fast & Furious 6 ===
YEAR: 2013
SOURCE: IMDb
SOURCE_URL: https://www.imdb.com/title/tt1905041/

## Trivia
Han dies in crash referencing Tokyo Drift timeline. Post-credits scene sets up Furious 7.

## Production Facts
Tank chase on Spanish highway among largest practical stunts in series.

## Casting Notes
Full ensemble returns. Gina Carano as Riley Hicks. Luke Evans as Owen Shaw.

---

=== MOVIE: Furious 7 ===
YEAR: 2015
SOURCE: IMDb
SOURCE_URL: https://www.imdb.com/title/tt2820852/

## Trivia
Completed after Paul Walker death using stand-ins and CGI. Song See You Again tied to tribute.

## Production Facts
Paul Walker died during filming; completed using brothers Caleb and Cody Walker and CGI.

## Casting Notes
Paul Walker final completed role. Jason Statham as Deckard Shaw. Kurt Russell as Mr. Nobody.

---

=== MOVIE: The Fate of the Furious ===
YEAR: 2017
SOURCE: IMDb
SOURCE_URL: https://www.imdb.com/title/tt4630562/

## Trivia
Charlize Theron improvised some Cipher lines. First film where Dom actively opposes family.

## Production Facts
Submarine chase and zombie-car sequences push absurdity further.

## Casting Notes
Charlize Theron as Cipher. Helen Mirren debuts in franchise.

---

=== MOVIE: F9: The Fast Saga ===
YEAR: 2021
SOURCE: IMDb
SOURCE_URL: https://www.imdb.com/title/tt5433138/

## Trivia
Han retcon survival explained via Mr. Nobody extraction. John Cena plays Dom's brother.

## Production Facts
Magnet-plane and rocket-car sequences. Justin Lin returns to direct.

## Casting Notes
John Cena as Jakob Toretto. Han returns alive, explaining his survival.

---

=== MOVIE: Fast X ===
YEAR: 2023
SOURCE: IMDb
SOURCE_URL: https://www.imdb.com/title/tt5433140/

## Trivia
Dante Reyes connects back to Fast Five villain Hernan Reyes. Planned two-part finale begins.

## Production Facts
Louis Leterrier replaced Justin Lin. Part one of planned two-part finale.

## Casting Notes
Jason Momoa as Dante Reyes. Brie Larson joins as Tess.
